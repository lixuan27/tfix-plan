Streaming video editing, pass through augmented reality in vehicles

How do we edit a live video stream without drift, from a temporal denoising point of view?

What

Restyle a camera stream in real time, chunk by chunk, for minutes at a time.

Why it is hard

Editing inside an autoregressive video model accumulates error, so the picture drifts away from the scene.

Our angle

Recast the task as flicker denoising plus temporal reconstruction, and let pretrained models carry the rest.

06   the idea

The image model edits. The video model fixes.

Six consecutive frames of one real per frame edit. Every car, sign and pole holds its place, while the frames keep disagreeing with one another. That disagreement is the entire remaining problem.

07   the system

One frozen model, one trained model. Click a stage.

Stage A, the editor stays frozen

An image editor runs on every frame at four distilled steps, around two seconds per frame. It keeps each car, sign and pole in place. Since it has no memory, drift has nowhere to accumulate.

A shared latent space

A video codec and a text encoder, both frozen. We measured the codec floor first and found it clean, so the flicker inside the draft is genuinely ours to remove.

Stage B, the only trained model

A causal video transformer, started from a distilled streaming generator. Draft and source enter through fresh channels that begin at zero, so training starts exactly at the pretrained model and improves from there. One denoising step at inference.

The runtime streams

Chunk by chunk, with a rolling cache of fixed size whose positions are re indexed to stay inside the trained range. Any stream length, constant memory, constant latency.

The output is watched, then measured

Blur, frozen motion and a reverted edit all please a flicker metric. So the video decides first, and the table confirms afterwards.

11   how much noise to teach

The reconstruction gives the right kind of flicker. We supply the right amount.

A reconstruction flickers the same way a real edit does, in spectrum and in colour, at roughly a third of the amount. Jitter that leaves appearance untouched covers the rest, so the clean clip stays the right answer.

The trap

A stronger prompt reaches the same amount by repainting the scene, and then the clean clip answers a different question. So we move the amount while appearance holds still.
